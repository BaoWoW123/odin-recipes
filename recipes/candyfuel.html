Candy Fuel

Ingredients

  • White sugar
  • Potassium Nitrate
      Also known as tree stump remover
  • That's it!

Steps

    1. Combine sugar and potassium nitrate in 50:50 weight ratio.
    2. Cook mixture on low heat for several minutes until caramel texture and slimy consistency.
    3. Put into container or pipe.
    4. Install fuse and let cool down for 30 minutes to an hour.
